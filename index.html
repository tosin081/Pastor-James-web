ABOUT PASTOR JAMES OLUWAFEMI

Pastor James Oluwafemi he is a Prophet and a minister of the gospel of Jesus Christ. He is the founder of world youth
christian movement and The senior Pastor of The latter Days Christian center

BIOGRAPHY

Pastor James Oluwafemi is born on June 3 1990, he is from Ibadan Oyo State, Nigeria.
he got converted at a very tender age as a muslim, since then he has been in the faith
